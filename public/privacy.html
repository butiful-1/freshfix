Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 4, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 4, 2026

Your privacy matters. We collect only what we need to operate Old2New and never sell your personal information to third parties.

1. Who We Are

Old2New ("we," "us," or "our") is an AI-powered recipe transformation service operated from Arizona, United States. We help you reimagine your favorite comfort recipes into healthier alternatives. Our website is old2new.app and you can reach us at support@old2new.app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: Email address and password (hashed) when you create an account.
  • Recipe data: Recipes and transformation preferences you submit to the app.
  • Usage data: Number of recipe transformations used, subscription plan, and feature interactions.
  • Payment information: Billing details processed securely by Stripe. We do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Device & log data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and pages visited, collected automatically via server logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and personalize the Old2New service, including AI-powered recipe transformations.
  • Manage your account, subscription, and usage quotas.
  • Process payments and send receipts.
  • Send transactional emails (e.g., account confirmation, password reset).
  • Improve our AI models and service quality using aggregated, anonymized data.
  • Respond to support requests and communications.
  • Comply with applicable laws and protect against fraud or abuse.

4. AI Processing — Anthropic API

Recipe transformations are powered by the Anthropic Claude AI API. When you submit a recipe, it is sent to Anthropic's servers for processing. Anthropic's use of this data is governed by their Privacy Policy. We do not share your account identity with Anthropic — only the recipe content is transmitted.

5. Data Storage — Supabase

Your account data, saved recipes, and transformation history are stored in a Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database. Supabase is SOC 2 Type II compliant and stores data in secure, encrypted environments. For more information, see Supabase's Privacy Policy.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Anthropic — to process recipe transformations via the Claude API.
  • Supabase — to store and manage your account and recipe data.
  • Stripe — to process subscription payments securely.
  • Law enforcement or regulators — if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of our users.

7. Subscription Plans & Data

Old2New offers the following subscription tiers:

  • Free — 5 recipe transformations per month, no charge.
  • Basic — $9.99/month, 50 recipe transformations per month.
  • Pro — $14.99/month, 150 recipe transformations per month.

Usage counts reset at the start of each billing cycle. Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe and is subject to their privacy practices.

8. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage and session cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or cross-site tracking.

9. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., dispute resolution, fraud prevention).

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • Data portability (receive your data in a machine-readable format).

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@old2new.app. We will respond within 30 days.

11. Children's Privacy

Old2New is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

12. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption in transit, hashed passwords, and access controls on our database. No system is 100% secure; if you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at support@old2new.app.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top and, for material changes, notify you by email or an in-app notice. Your continued use of Old2New after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
